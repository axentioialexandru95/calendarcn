## Starter Surface

<CalendarExample id="basic" />

Use this version when you want the smallest controlled integration: separate
surface and toolbar primitives, local state, optimistic updates, and direct
event creation without the bundled overlays.

- Best for your first install of `calendar-core` + `calendar-toolbar`
- Good base for product scheduling or internal team calendars
- Keeps all views available so users can switch between month, week, day, and agenda
