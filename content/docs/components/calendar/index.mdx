## Notes

- `CalendarRoot` is fully controlled. Keep `date`, `view`, and `events` in your
  own state or wire them into your store.
- The utility helpers in `@/components/calendar/utils` are the fastest path to
  local optimistic handlers for move, resize, and create flows.
- `resources`, `businessHours`, and `blockedRanges` are optional. The same core
  interaction model works with or without them.
- Use `eventChangeConfirmation` when your app needs to gate schedule changes
  behind a dialog or persistence check.
